Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 9, 2026

HirePilot helps users analyze job fit, improve resume text, generate cover letters, and prepare for interviews using AI-assisted tools. This Privacy Policy explains how information may be handled when you use the app.

Information You Provide

You may enter or import resume text, profile information, job descriptions, cover letter content, and interview preparation content. This information is used to provide HirePilot features and generate AI-assisted outputs.

AI Processing

HirePilot may send resume text, job descriptions, and related prompts to AI service providers, including OpenAI, to generate analysis, resume improvements, cover letters, and interview preparation content. Do not submit information you do not want processed by these services.

Local Storage

Saved analyses and saved resumes may be stored locally on your device using app storage. HirePilot does not currently provide account sync, iCloud sync, or server-side saved history for these items.

Subscriptions and Purchases

Subscription purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. HirePilot does not receive or store your full payment card details.

Analytics and Diagnostics

HirePilot may use basic app diagnostics or analytics to understand app performance, improve reliability, and identify issues. Any analytics should be used to improve the product and not to sell your personal data.

No Sale of Personal Data

HirePilot does not sell your resume text, job descriptions, or personal information.

No Employment Guarantee

HirePilot provides AI-assisted career tools, but it does not guarantee interviews, job offers, hiring outcomes, salary outcomes, or employment results.

Your Responsibility

You are responsible for reviewing, editing, and verifying all AI-generated content before using it in job applications or professional communications.
